Some history
It all started with the very first stone in our pillar of cocktails: "Mama Franklin". She was an American nurse who, back in 1917, came to help in World War I. Besides aiding the many soldiers fallen front battlefield, she also had a knack for making drinks. Rest assured, no soldier complained and many returned, even after the war was over.

Due to an issue with the boats, Mama Franklin was left stranded on the very beaches of Normandy. Earning a living as nomadic bar maiden, she arrived in Belgium.

What more is there to say? Nightly cocktails was born!
Many years later, we now run a proud company with the same flexibily as Mama Franklin. So try us out! Wether you are a modern day fallen soldier or not, no soul on this planet should be left alone without a drink.
